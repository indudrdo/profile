Profile

A distinguished scientist of national repute with over 35 years of leadership experience in advanced research, scientific administration, and institutional governance. Superannuated from the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) as Scientist 'G', the highest scientist grade, after contributing extensively to mission-critical national programmes.

Career began at Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL), working on radar technologies, and subsequently joined DRDO in 1986. Over an accomplished tenure, held important scientific and managerial roles across premier DRDO establishments including SSPL, Dte of Standardization, DRDO Headquarters, LASTEC, and CEPTAM.

A rare blend of technical expertise, policy insight, administrative leadership, and strategic decision-making.
